    Treatments for speech impediment


     Speech therapy can help if a delay in development affects speech.
     Speech therapy is a helpful part of treatment for those who have
     conditions such as autism, cerebral palsy or hearing loss. Some speech impediments may require surgery.


     Prevention

            
       Most speech impediments cannot be predicted or prevented. Talk to your healthcare provider if you are 
       struggling to speak or not speaking. They can assess your child's speech, identify any
       potential problems and recommend steps you could take.

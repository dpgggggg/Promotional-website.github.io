     Can you treat speech impediment? 


 Different types of speech impediment requires different treatment. 
Usually, speech therapy could help for those whose condition isn't related to other conditions
and early treatment is always the most effective. Contact a healthcare proffesional if you or someone you 
know has speech impediment for treatment.
